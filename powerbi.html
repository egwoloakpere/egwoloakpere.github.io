POWER BI PROJECTS

Power BI is a powerful business analytics tool developed by Microsoft. It allows users to visualize and analyze data from a variety of sources, such as databases, Excel files, and online services, to gain insights into their business. With Power BI, users can create interactive reports, dashboards, and visualizations that can be easily shared and accessed across their organization. Power BI provides a wide range of tools and features, including data modeling, data preparation, and data visualization, making it a comprehensive solution for business intelligence needs. These projects involves; Identify the needs of users, Using Power BI to connect to data sources and transform them into meaningful insights, create compelling reports and dashboards to visualize and report insights and use design thinking to improve dashboards.
